# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 14, 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains how SnapLink, Inc. ("SnapLink", "we") collects,
uses, and shares information when you use the SnapLink desktop app, website,
and related services (together, the "Service").

Plain-English summary at the top. The legal language below is a complement to
it, not a replacement.

## TL;DR

- **What we store**: your screenshots, their OCR text, your account email, and
  minimal metadata.
- **Why**: to serve the URLs, run OCR, manage your subscription, and detect
  abuse.
- **What we don't do**: sell your data, share it with advertisers, or train
  models on your screenshots.
- **Retention**: uploads follow the expiration you set (`never`, `1h`, `1d`,
  `7d`, `30d`). Account data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
- **Where**: primary region is the US (Cloudflare R2 and AWS us-east-1). EU
  residency is available on the Team plan.

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide

- **Account information**: email address, subscription status, billing
  metadata (we never see your card; Stripe does).
- **Uploaded content**: the screenshots you upload and any metadata you add
  (filename, tags).
- **Support correspondence**: messages you send to `support@snaplink.io`.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

- **Device metadata**: OS, SnapLink version, non-identifying device hash.
  This is used to authenticate your client and detect abuse.
- **Usage telemetry** (opt-in only): anonymized feature usage. Off by default.
- **Crash reports** (opt-in only): stack traces with redacted file paths. Off
  by default.
- **Upload metadata**: size, dimensions, MIME type, timestamp, IP hash.

### 1.3 What we deliberately don't collect

- We do **not** run third-party analytics on the website or the app by
  default.
- We do **not** collect your browsing history, file system contents, or any
  data outside the screenshot folders you configure.
- We do **not** read the contents of files you haven't uploaded.
- We do **not** train models on your screenshots, OCR text, or usage.

## 2. How we use information

- **Provide the Service**: store and serve screenshots, run OCR, manage
  sessions, enforce plan limits.
- **Communicate**: transactional email (magic links, receipts, security
  alerts). We don't send marketing email unless you opt in.
- **Prevent abuse**: detect and remove content that violates our AUP (see
  Terms), including automated CSAM scanning.
- **Improve the Service**: aggregated, anonymized insights — only if you
  opt in.

## 3. How we share information

- **Sub-processors** (see §7): infrastructure providers that process data
  under strict DPAs.
- **Legal compliance**: if we receive a valid legal demand, we comply after
  scrutiny and will notify you unless legally prohibited.
- **Safety**: we share reports of CSAM with NCMEC as required by law.
- **Never with advertisers**. We don't run ads on the Service.

## 4. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, UK-GDPR, and similar), you have
the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate data.
- **Delete** your account and associated data.
- **Port** your data to another service.
- **Object** to certain processing.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time for opt-in processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email
<a href="mailto:privacy@snaplink.io">privacy@snaplink.io</a>. We respond
within 30 days.

## 5. Data retention

| Data class | Retention |
|---|---|
| Screenshots | Per upload's expiration setting (default: `never` on Pro, 7 days on Free). Deleted permanently on expiration. |
| OCR text | Same as its parent screenshot. |
| Account data | Until account deletion, then deleted within 30 days. |
| Security logs (auth, abuse) | 90 days. |
| Billing records | As required by tax law (typically 7 years). |

## 6. Security

- TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest.
- Authentication via magic link + device-scoped JWT. Tokens stored in the OS
  keychain.
- Password-less by design — there is no password to steal.
- Annual third-party penetration test. SOC 2 Type II in progress.

Report vulnerabilities to <a href="mailto:security@snaplink.io">security@snaplink.io</a>.
PGP fingerprint: `B7 1E 3F A2 8C 4D 9E 60  11 DA 5C E8 07 F4 2B 95`.

## 7. Sub-processors

| Provider | Purpose | Data residency |
|---|---|---|
| Cloudflare R2 | Screenshot storage and CDN | Global, primary US |
| Cloudflare Workers | Edge auth / rate limit | Global |
| AWS (us-east-1) | Application servers, Postgres, Redis | US |
| Stripe | Subscription payments | US |
| Resend | Transactional email (magic links, receipts) | US |
| Sentry | Crash reporting (opt-in) | US |
| PostHog | Usage analytics (opt-in) | US (self-hosted EU option on Team) |

We'll update this list at least 30 days before adding a new sub-processor
that handles personal data.

## 8. International transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States. Transfers rely on
Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required.

## 9. Children

SnapLink is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
information from children under 13.

## 10. Changes

We'll post changes on this page with a new "Effective date." Material
changes will be emailed to active users at least 14 days before taking
effect.
